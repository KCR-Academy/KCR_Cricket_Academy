Expert Led Coaching

Our mission is to deliver holistic training that integrates technical skill-building, tactical guidance, character development, and mentorship, fostering an environment where young cricketers can reach their fullest potential. Through expert coaching, intensive practice, and a commitment to personal integrity, we aspire to develop athletes who excel on the field, inspire their peers, and promote a positive, community-focused cricket culture.

Developing talented cricketers

Our vision is to nurture talented cricketers of strong character through world-class technical and professional training, empowering them to excel in cricket, inspire others, and positively impact their communities.

Coach

Deepali Rokade - Coach Deepali is an ICC Level 2 (ECB) Coach, whose playing career saw her represent Maharashtra , West Zone and India at U15 and U19 levels and Womens National team.   Coach Deepali was captain of India Junior Girls team.  As captain of Maharasthra, Ms. Deepali Rokade led her side to the national championship twice, an unbroken record in women's cricket.  In her coaching career, Coach Deepali coached India U16 boys at the Commonwealth Cricket Series, where the Indian boys won Silver.  Coach Deepali also coached Maharashtra U19 girls during 2006 and 2007 and was a selector of MCA Junior and Senior women's teams.  Coach Deepali is a recipient of the Best Coach award from MCA for her services.  Coach Deepali has a M.Sc. in Sports from Christ Church (Oxford University) and a Diploma in Sports Coaching (specializing in cricket) from the National Institute of Sports, India.
